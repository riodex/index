This is a graphical user interface for some good ethereum smart contracts.

Click on red words to get a graphical user helper for the corresponding actions. (Pink words are feature teasers. They don't do anything when you click them.)

Once an action has been set up, click the "act!" word to request your client to sign and broadcast a transaction. Click the grey "back" word to quit the helper without generating a transaction.

The help action is used to donate 0.123 Ether to the riodex.art developers. Who's helping who?

Remember, click on the word "back" if you do not want to sign and broadcast this Ethereum transaction.
